# Classic Spaghetti Bolognese Guide

This guide provides a structured approach to preparing a traditional spaghetti bolognese, focusing on building deep flavors through proper technique and fresh ingredients.

## 1. Ingredients and Preparation

Before starting the cooking process, ensure all ingredients are prepped (mise en place) to ensure a smooth workflow.

### 1.1 Ingredient List

```yaml
Base:
  - 500g Spaghetti
  - 500g Ground Beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  - 2 tbsp Olive Oil
Vegetables (Mirepoix):
  - 1 Large Onion, finely diced
  - 2 Carrots, finely minced
  - 2 Celery stalks, finely minced
  - 3 Cloves Garlic, crushed
Liquids and Seasoning:
  - 800g Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  - 2 tbsp Tomato Paste
  - 125ml Dry Red Wine
  - 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  - Salt and Black Pepper to taste
```

## 2. Cooking Instructions

### 2.1 Sautéing the Aromatics

Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery. Cook for 8-10 minutes until the vegetables are softened and translucent. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

### 2.2 Browning the Meat

Increase the heat to medium-high. Add the ground beef to the pot, breaking it up with a wooden spoon. Cook until the meat is thoroughly browned. Drain excess fat if necessary.

### 2.3 Deglazing and Simmering

Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes to caramelize. Pour in the red wine to deglaze the pan, scraping up any browned bits (fond) from the bottom. Add the crushed tomatoes and oregano. Reduce heat to low and simmer for at least 30-45 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

## 3. Pasta Assembly

### 3.1 Boiling the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until it reaches an 'al dente' texture.

### 3.2 Final Integration

```bash
# Pro-tip: Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining
# Combine the pasta directly with the sauce
# Use the reserved water to emulsify the sauce if it is too thick
```

Serve immediately with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a garnish of basil.
